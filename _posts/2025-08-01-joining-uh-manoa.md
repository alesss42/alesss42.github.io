I am thrilled to join the Department of Oceanography at the University of Hawaiʻi at Mānoa as an Assistant Professor starting August 2025. 

I will be building the Ocean Mixing and Acoustics Lab, where we will study fine-scale ocean structure, turbulence, and the connection between ocean physics and acoustics.

I am actively recruiting PhD and Master's students — if you are interested, please visit the [students page](/students/) for more information.
