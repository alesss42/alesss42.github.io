## Prospective Students

Thank you for your interest in our lab! I am currently recruiting **PhD and Master's students** for **Spring 2027** and **Fall 2028**.

If you are interested in physical oceanography, ocean mixing, acoustics, and observational oceanography, I encourage you to reach out. Please send me an email with:

- Your CV
- A brief description of your research interests
- Why you want to pursue a PhD or Master's degree and why you are interested in our lab

Students are funded through a combination of:

- **Teaching Assistantship (TA)** — students are expected to assist in undergraduate courses
- **Research Assistantship (RA)** — students contribute to research efforts through fieldwork and/or data analysis
